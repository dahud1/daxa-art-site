FAQs

How do I commission a portrait?

It's easy! Simply upload a clear photo of your subject on our website, and complete the payment process. We will then get to work creating your stunning digital art portrait.

How long does it take to receive my final digital art image?

The turnaround time varies depending on the complexity and size of the portrait, but typically deliver the final image within 3-5 business days.

Can I use the digital art image for commercial purposes?

No, the digital art images are for personal use only. This means that you can print the image for personal use, but you cannot use it for commercial gain or reproduction.

What if I'm not satisfied with the final digital art image?

We want you to be completely satisfied with your portrait. If you're not happy with the final image, you can request one free revisions. Any additional revisions will require aditional payment.

How do I pay for my commission?

Payments are made securely through Stripe. We don't store any information about your card. In addition, Stripe has excellent bank-level security standards.

Can I make changes to my order after I've placed it?

It depends on the changes. If you need to make minor adjustments, please use the contact email as soon as possible. If significant changes are needed, additional fees may apply.

Do you offer refunds?

We do not offer refunds once work has begun on your portrait. However, we will work with you to ensure you are completely satisfied with the final product.

What quality of image do I need to provide?

To ensure the best quality of your digital art portrait, we recommend providing a high-resolution image of at least 300 dpi. If the image is low quality, it may impact the final product.

Can I commission a portrait of more than one person?

Our digital art portraits are typically of a single person. If you have a specific request to includemultiple people, please contact us prior to placing your order. Also, if the image you provide contains more than one person, please let us know which person you want to have drawn.

Can I request a specific style for my portrait?

The final piece will be similar to the examples. If you have a specific request for a particular style or technique, please let us know in the order notes, and we will do our best to accommodate your request.

What size options do you offer for digital art portraits?

We offer one size: 2000 x 2300 pixels. This is high quality enough to be used oneline and for print.

Can I use my digital art portrait for social media or personal websites?

Yes, you can use your digital art portrait for personal use, including social media and personal websites. However, please note that the image cannot be used for commercial purposes.
